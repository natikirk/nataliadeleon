Hi there!


I’m a Dipl. Architect from the TU Simón Bolívar in Caracas, Venezuela.

I developed interest on a cross disciplinary culture of computational design and the art of manufacturing. How humans, machines and codes co-exist. I specialized in creative technology, computational design, 3d printers and digital fabrication.

I'm curious, playful and inquisitive collaborative, with a keen eye for design and development.
