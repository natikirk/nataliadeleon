Hi there!


I'm an experimental architect from Venezuela based in Berlin.

My practice drifts in between art, programing & design, exploring morphologies as generative & adaptive concepts, where the line between humans, machines, and codes begin to blur.

I specialized in creative technology, computational design, 3d printers and digital fabrication.

I'm curious, playful and inquisitive collaborative, with a keen eye for design and development.

XXV0

When high-end design dives into the aquarium

Client: Manglar Spaces

Freelance Work. 3D Modelling and conceptual design

@ Madrid, Spain

We can physically feel matter. We can touch and hold it. And although we may not always be able to see or hear it, we know that it exists. Matter of Growth is an experience of physical data in the form of a life-sized sculpture, designed to foster a sense of wonder and connection with the world around us.

Take a moment to imagine the evolution of a living organism. DNA is the blueprint from which all species are created, and it stores every single piece of information about each form of life.

As DNA, the algorithm designed to find this form, recreates itself and is capable of mutating.

Each module created from this DNA-Data Storage can adapt to one another and evolve together until it can finally be immersed in our tangible world.

This is an attempt to reveal what we cannot see and unearth what is behind a Matter.

This is also meant to show how beautiful the language of math could be.

# It is all made by patterns, and we are getting closer to revealing them.

This is an interpretation of how Matter is made.

Bayan Wall

World's first fully Additively Manufactured (AM) green wall

Alongside: NOWLAB, BigRep Gmbh, Innovation Department, Digital Solutions

@ Berlin, Germany - 2019

The Bayan Eco Wall is the world's first fully Additively Manufactured (AM) green wall with an innovative embedded water supply and drainage system. More than simply aesthetic, the bionic design is structurally customized thanks to algorithm design tools, and the plant carriers organically snap into place.

Free from traditional production, distribution, and consumption processes. Its printed put of recycled plastic on-site, actively supporting biodiversity in urban environments and environmentally conscious approaches to design and technology.

Exhibit alongside Tangible Collective: AS BELOW SO ABOVE (2020 -2021)

@ Willow Gallery, Wilhelmshavener Str.47, 10551 Berlin, Germany

Xeno-Culture of life refers to the fear of the alien in the world. This fear we have been accustomed to anesthetized our imagination.

A gene is the basic physical and functional unit of heredity, characteristics that make you unique. Genes mutate and survive in different shapes.

By intensifying new thresholds, this xeno-gene has materialized to seduce, unlock, and let loose a hint of the Sublime and the Unknown into a new form that doesn't yet exist.

Lattice Shoe

Reverse engineering for a printabe sole

Alongside: NOWLAB, BigRep Gmbh, Innovation Department, Digital Solutions

@Berlin, Germany - 2019

Client: BASF

A printable sole (SLA Printer) generated through algorithm-aided and parametric design provides a model that can act according to a specific pressure, weight, and size data. It also allows users to customize the functionality according to their needs.

It is specially conceived to increase usability and performance for runners, making it a high-quality product with a high-tech resolution.
